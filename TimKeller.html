Timothy Keller

Photo taken from thegospelcoalition.org

Tim Keller is the former pastor of Redeemer Presbyterian Church in Manhattan. Since he founded the church in 1989, it has grown from 50 to 5,000 regular attendees. Although he has recently stepped down as the lead pastor of Redeemer Presbyterian Church, he is still very involved in his community, training the next generation of church leaders as well as planting churches through Redeemer City to City.

  • 1950: Born in Pennsylvania
  • 1972: Graduated Bucknell Universitye
  • 1975: Received his Master's Degree from Gordon-Conwell Theological Seminary
  • 1981: Received his Doctorate from Westminister Theological Seminary
  • 1989: Founded Redeemer Presbyterian Church
  • 2017: Currently lives in Manhattan in New York City
